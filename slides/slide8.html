What is n8n?

Our automation platform that works 24/7 gathering data and running processes automatically

Key Point: This is OUR infrastructure

  • Hosted on our Azure environment (not external servers)
  • Running on our own domain: n8n.gfgam.com
  • Connected directly to our database
  • Fully controlled and customized for our needs

Impact:

  • 18,000+ records processed automatically
  • Runs every week without human intervention
  • 95% reduction in data entry errors
  • Frees our team to focus on analysis, not data collection

What It Does Automatically:

  • Collects data directly from source systems and ingests it into our database
  • Also processes Excel files from SharePoint for legacy data
  • Gathers market intelligence (competitor pricing, reviews via APIFY, SERP API & ScrapingBee)
  • Monitors digital presence across booking platforms (Google, TripAdvisor, Booking, Expedia)
  • Validates and cleans data before storing it
  • Sends alerts when something needs attention
