The Technology Leap: Data Management

From Spreadsheets to Professional Database

BEFORE: The Old Way

"Server":

OneDrive folders (cloud storage, not a real server)

"Database":

Excel spreadsheets scattered across files

Problems:

  • Manual consolidation taking hours every week
  • High risk of errors and data inconsistencies
  • No real-time information
  • Cannot handle growth beyond 15-20 properties

NOW: The Professional Way

Server:

Microsoft Azure (enterprise cloud platform)

Database:

PostgreSQL (industry-standard database system)

Benefits:

  • Automatic data consolidation - no manual work
  • Single source of truth - zero inconsistencies
  • Real-time information available 24/7
  • Can easily scale to 50+ properties

The Numbers Tell the Story:

2019 to mid-2025: ~17,000 records collected manually (6 years)

Since migration (1 month): ~40,000 records collected automatically

Result: More data in 1 month than 6 years combined - exponentially better analysis and service quality
